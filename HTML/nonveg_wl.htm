Non-veg foods are high in protein content which is an essential element for smooth functioning and healing of the body. The animal proteins contain all the amino acids, ensuring complete nourishment, which is often lacking in case of most of the plant based proteins. Lack of protein in body can lead to a number of major health complications including weakness, leathery, muscle loss, inability to concentrate and many more.

Non-veg foods, like meat and poultry work as the primary source of iron in the body. Iron is highly important not only to keep blood deficiency at bay but also to ensure smooth functioning of the brain.

Below are some suggestions for breakfast, lunch, evening snack and dinner for a non-vegetarian diet plan for weight loss.



Start Losing Weight In Just One Week -


Monday-

Early morning - (6 am)
~ Start your day early with lemon water (you can add honey). This will help the body to get rid of harmful acids produced by the body.

1. Breakfast - (8 am)
~ Two fruits like apple or banana, 1 cup of tea.

2. Lunch - (1 pm)
~ Replace white carbohydrates like rice, bread, potatoes or pasta with whole wheat bread or chapattis, 1 piece of chicken with salad or fruit.

3. Evening snack - (4 pm)
~ Egg and mayo sandwich: 2 slices of brown bread filled with slices of boiled egg, sliced tomatoes, and low-fat mayo.

4. Dinner - (8 pm)
~ Fish, steamed broccoli or any leafy vegetables and fat free ice cream.


Tuesday

1. Breakfast - (8 am)
~ 1 bowl Poha or Upma or Idli.

2. Lunch - (1 pm)
~ 2 chapattis or a small bowl of rice with vegetables.

3. Evening snack - (4 pm)
~ 5 Digestive biscuits with tea (Green tea is highly recommended for weight loss and health.)

4. Dinner- (8 pm)
~ Light dinner of salad or a whole wheat bread sandwich.

Wednesday

1. Breakfast - (8 am)
~ 1 bowl of unsweetened muesli with skimmed milk, a slice of multigrain bread and 1 orange.

2. Lunch - (1 pm)
~ 1 cup rice, and dal, vegetable curry, any fruits like grapes, or bananas.

3. Evening snack - (4 pm)
~ Vegetable crudités with low-fat cheese.

4. Dinner - (8 pm)
~ Vegetable curry, chapattis, and baked or steamed potato.

Thursday

1. Breakfast - (8 am)
~ Fruits like apples, banana, and dahi.

2. Lunch - (1 pm)
~ Tomato soup, roasted chicken, radish, lettuce, tomato, pear and whole wheat chapattis with 1 cup of chopped vegetables.

3. Evening snack - (4 pm)
~ 1 bowl of fruit salad and 1 cup of low-fat yogurt.

4. Dinner - (8 pm)
~ Cooked brown rice and pineapple/apple/orange pieces.

Friday

1. Breakfast - (8 am)
~ 1/2 cup tomatoes, a few almonds.

2. Lunch - (1 pm)
~ Fat free beans and 1 chapatti with vegetables and cucumber slices.

3. Evening snack - (4 pm)
~ 1 small can of baked beans, grilled mushrooms and 3 slices of brown bread.

4. Dinner - (8 pm)
~ Green salad with fat-free dressing, chapattis.

Saturday

1. Breakfast - (8 am)
~ 1 bowl of instant porridge topped with chopped fresh fruits and 1 boiled egg.

2. Lunch - (1 pm)
~ 1 chapatti, 1 cup of rice, light yoghurt, fish curry, mustard, cucumber, tomatoes and onion slices.

3. Evening snack - (4 pm)
~ Puffed rice with tea or coffee.

4. Dinner - (8 pm)
~ Brown rice, steamed spinach and an apple.

Sunday

1. Breakfast - (8 am)
~ 1 bowl Poha or Upma or Idli, ½ a bowl of sliced tomatoes.

2. Lunch - (1 pm)
~ Rice, dal,salads.

3. Evening snack - (4 pm)
~ 1 cup of sprouts with pomegranate arils and tea.

4. Dinner - (8 pm)
~ Grilled chicken, chapattis, pineapple slices.
