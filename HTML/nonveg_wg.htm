Non-veg foods are high in protein content which is an essential element for smooth functioning and healing of the body. The animal proteins contain all the amino acids, ensuring complete nourishment, which is often lacking in case of most of the plant based proteins. Lack of protein in body can lead to a number of major health complications including weakness, leathery, muscle loss, inability to concentrate and many more.

Discarding non-veg foods from your diet can easily give rise to vitamin and mineral deficiencies in your body which can lead to a range of health issues like fatigue, depression, etc.
Below are some food items that you should include in your daily diet in order to increase your calorie intake-

  • Oysters
  • Salmon
  • Chicken Breast
  • Crabs
